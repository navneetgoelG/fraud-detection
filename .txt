**Credit Card Fraud Detection**
*Anonymized credit card transactions labeled as fradulent or genuine*

*Inspiration*

Identify fradulent credit card transactions.
Given the class imbalance ratio, we recommend measuring the accuracy using the Area Under the precision-Recall Curve (AUPRC). 
Confusion matrix accuracy is not meaningful for unbalanced classification.

See here for the link to the data: [https://www.kaggle.com/mlg-ulb/creditcardfraud]
